### Content and Padding

The `CardContent` component is used to add padding around a `Card`'s content.

```js
<Card>
  <CardContent>
    <Text size="4">Padding</Text>
    <Text size="5" mode="subdued">Some more text</Text>
  </CardContent>
</Card>
```
